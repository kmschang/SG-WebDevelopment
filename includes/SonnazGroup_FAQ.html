Q: What is Sonnaz Group, LLC?

 

A: Sonnaz Group, LLC is an independent app development company focused on creating simple, elegant mobile and web applications. We design and develop apps that solve problems efficiently without overcomplicating things. Our goal is to create tools that make life easier in the most intuitive way possible.

Q: What services does Sonnaz Group, LLC offer?

 

A: At Sonnaz Group, LLC, we specialize in mobile app development for iOS and web development. We focus on creating clean, user-friendly apps that get the job done. Whether you're looking for a unique mobile experience or a web-based solution, we aim to deliver something simple and elegant.

Q: Who do you build apps for?

 

A: As an indie developer, I create apps primarily for myself, but I share them to help others with similar needs. I don't create apps for clients or businesses, but my apps are designed to be useful to anyone looking for easy, effective solutions.

Q: Which platforms do you develop for?

 

A: Currently, we focus on iOS development. Our apps are built with the intention of providing seamless experiences for Apple users, but we also create web apps that are accessible across platforms.

Q: What makes Sonnaz Group's apps different from others?

 

A: Our apps stand out because we don't try to solve everything with a single app. Each of our creations is simple and elegant, designed to do just enough to meet a specific need. We value functionality without unnecessary features, offering a clean and effective user experience.

Q: Do you offer custom app development?

 

A: At this time, we do not offer custom app development services. Our focus is on creating apps that serve our own needs, which we share with the community. However, we believe our approach and the apps we develop could be helpful to many.

Q: How long does it take to develop an app?

 

A: Developing an app typically takes a few months, but the work doesn't stop there. We continue to add new features and improvements based on feedback and evolving needs, so the apps are always a work in progress.

Q: What is the pricing model for your apps?

 

A: Currently, all of our apps are free. We are focused on delivering value and simplicity, but in the future, we may switch to a one-time payment model for premium features or new releases.

Q: Do you offer maintenance and support for your apps?

 

A: Since our apps are made for personal use and development, we do not offer official support or maintenance services. However, we do make updates regularly to improve functionality and user experience based on feedback.
