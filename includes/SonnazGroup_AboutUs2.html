Our Story

Sonnaz Group was founded in February 2024 with a simple mission: to create apps that make life easier. It all started with a project for my mom—an idea that quickly turned into a passion for building practical, user-friendly applications. While still in school, I began taking requests, turning one small project into something much bigger. Today, I’m working on three apps simultaneously, continuously pushing the boundaries of what’s possible. Though I’m the only full-time developer, Sonnaz Group is a team effort, with four dedicated collaborators who contribute whenever they can. Together, we’re committed to innovation, problem-solving, and building technology that truly helps people.
