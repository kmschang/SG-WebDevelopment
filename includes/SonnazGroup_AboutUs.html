Our Story

Sonnaz Group was founded in February 2024 with a simple goal: to create apps that make life easier. What began as a personal project—building an app for my mom—quickly turned into a passion for developing useful, intuitive software. While balancing school, I started taking requests, each new project reinforcing my love for problem-solving and design. Before long, I realized this wasn’t just a hobby—it was a calling.

 

Today, Sonnaz Group is dedicated to designing simple, innovative apps that enhance lives, improve productivity, and protect privacy. I’m currently working on three apps, each focused on solving real-world problems with efficiency and elegance. Though I’m the only full-time developer, Sonnaz Group is supported by a small but talented team of four, who contribute their skills whenever they can. Together, we push the boundaries of technology to ensure that the solutions we create are accessible, user-friendly, and meaningful.

Our Mission

At Sonnaz Group, our mission is to design simple, innovative apps that enhance lives, improve productivity, and protect privacy. We believe technology should work for people—not the other way around. By focusing on intuitive design and practical solutions, we create software that seamlessly integrates into daily life, empowering users to accomplish more with less effort. Our commitment to accessibility ensures that our apps remain user-friendly, while our dedication to privacy means that security is always a priority. As we continue to grow, our goal remains the same: to build technology that makes a difference, giving people the tools they need to focus on what truly matters.
