Our Story

Sonnaz Group started in 2024 as an independent app studio built around one idea: make apps I actually want to use every day. Instead of operating as a client services firm, the focus is on creating and refining in-house products that stay practical, fast, and privacy-conscious.

 

Development is led by one full-time builder, with support from a small in-person team that contributes testing, feedback, and product perspective. That close collaboration keeps each release grounded in real daily use and helps turn rough ideas into polished tools people can rely on.
