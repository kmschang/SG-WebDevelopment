Day Tracker - Your Personal Time Companion

Stay in sync with time like never before with Day Tracker, the ultimate app that keeps you connected to every moment of the year. With Day Tracker, you’re always in control of time—whether it’s tracking the year, calculating days, or simply finding out what’s next on the calendar. Keep track of life’s precious moments effortlessly with this powerful yet simple tool.

Features:

  • Track the day of the year, week, and month.
  • Calculate the time difference between any two dates.
  • Find out what day/week/month any arbitrary date falls on.
  • Custom themes and badges for a personalized look and feel.
  • Easy-to-use and intuitive interface.

Day Tracker is your go-to app for everything time-related. Stay organized, informed, and ahead of the curve!

Today View

Track the Day: Instantly know what day it is in the year, month, or week. With a simple glance, you’ll see things like "Day 66 of 365," helping you stay aligned with your goals, milestones, or personal projects.

Time Machine

Navigate to Any Day: Wondering what day of the week a specific past, present, or future date falls on? Simply input the date and Day Tracker will tell you exactly where it lands.

Duration

Time Between Dates: Need to calculate the time between two dates? Day Tracker makes it easy, providing the difference in seconds, minutes, hours, days, weeks, or even years. Whether you're counting down to a big event or reflecting on time passed, it's all at your fingertips.

Settings

Customizable Experience: Tailor Day Tracker to your unique style with customizable themes and badges, so your app feels like it’s made just for you. Choose from various colors, layouts, and options to personalize the app to your preference.
